LIKE FOLLOW SUBSCRIBE 2k 24

October 10,11,12 2024

About Our Event

The Inter Collegiate Prayer Fellowship joyfully invites you to partake in the divine celebration of our 20th Annual Youth Camp 2024. This sacred gathering, graced with the theme LIKE FOLLOW SUBSCRIBE, promises to be a journey of profound spiritual renewal and heartfelt connection. We warmly embrace you to join us in this sacred space, where your presence will surely enhance our collective quest for enlightenment and grace. With open hearts and blessings, we eagerly await your participation in this transformative experience.

Agenda What to Expect

  • Opening Prayer: A moment to start the event with a prayer, setting a positive tone.
  • Revealing Divided the Students Like a Team: Students will be divided into teams and introduced, fostering collaboration and networking.
  • Time Schedule: Detailed schedule of the day's events will be shared, including timings and key activities.
  • Bible Meditation: Guided session on how to meditate on the Bible.
  • How to Meditate Bible: Practical tips and techniques for effective Bible meditation.
  • Action Song: Interactive song session to energize participants.
  • Special Guest Message: Bible message from a special guest.
  • Special Guest Worship: Engaging Worship led by the special guest.
  • Games: Fun and engaging games to foster interaction and enjoyment.
  • Prayer: Concluding the day with a prayer session.
  • Bible Meditation: Continued Bible meditation session.
  • Action Song: Another interactive song session.
  • Message: Bible message delivered to all participants.
  • Worship: An engaging Worship session for all attendees.
  • Prize Distribution: Distribution of prizes to winners of various activities.
  • Feedback Session: Gathering feedback from participants to improve future events.
